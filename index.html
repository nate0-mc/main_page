Website Information

The reason I created this website is so that I can share information and educate. Mathematics is one of my passions, and I want to show that passion by educating. I also want to share my work; the many mathematics guides I have made for my classmates can't just go to waste. I spend a minimum two weeks preparing these reviewers, so I can't imagine just using them only once and never again.

That's why this website was made. I want to: a) educate people, b) to share my work, and c) to document my life. My blog exists just so I can share my experiences to at least a crowd of two people.
