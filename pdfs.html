Information

This page serves as my hub for all the work I have compiled with \(\LaTeX\).

It is one of my favorite hobbies to typeset \(\LaTeX\) and I have been doing it for over a year. I consder myself an above-average beginner when it comes to it. The documents I have typeset over the long-run have been incredibly time-consuming but satisfying to work on. This page is to share these documents.

I have three kinds of documents listed here:

  1. Notes: Self-explanatory. They are from my self-studies. So far, I have not released any of these because my notes are a mess and are hard to grasp fully. However, I am currently recompiling all my messy notes from College Algebra to one document.

  2. Guides: These are more detailed notes. They are more-so to explain in a hopefully more simpler manner. Due to this, they can take up to 60 pages per quarter. Generally, these are for reviewing and practicing for a quarterly exam.
